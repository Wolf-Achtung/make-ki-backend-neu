Transformation Formula: 10‑20‑70

BCG’s rule of thumb for AI transformation: 10% algorithms, 20% technology & data, 70% people & processes. Treat AI primarily as a change challenge.

Stakeholder Map

Map interest vs. influence and tailor communication:

  • Low interest / low influence → passive information.
  • High interest / low influence → active information & involvement.
  • Low interest / high influence → active information & satisfaction.
  • High interest / high influence → key actors to be tightly involved.
