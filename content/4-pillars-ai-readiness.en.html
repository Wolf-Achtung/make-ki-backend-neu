4 Pillars of AI Readiness

Executive takeaway: Start where you are, prioritise quick wins and build a pragmatic, auditable operating model.

1) Governance & Compliance

Policies, roles, registry and DPIA are non‑negotiable.

2) Security & Risk

Minimise leakage, misuse and supply‑chain risks; build resilience.

3) Value & Processes

Prioritise measurable value with a lean operating model and quality gates.

4) Enablement & Culture

Role profiles, training paths, communication kit and success stories.
